At , we are committed to educating our members about financial matters so they can make more informed choices about managing their money and creating financial security for themselves and their families. Through educational seminars provided throughout the year, we offer the answers to many of the questions members ask regarding a variety of personal finance topics.

These seminars are designed to be educational and offer a no-obligation avenue for you to learn more about how to better provide for your financial security. Our network of registered financial advisors made available from Financial Network provide information you can depend on at every stage of your life.
  • Getting Started: It's never too late to begin mapping out your financial plan.
  • Achieving Your Goals: Car. Home. College. Retirement. These are just some of the common financial goals you might set for yourself.
  • Life Events: You just never know what will happen. Plan ahead so you'll be prepared for those unexpected life events.
  • Preserving Your Wealth: You've worked hard for your money. Learn how to maintain control, even in retirement or after your death.

Seminars are arranged by a single topic or by life stage, depending on member interest. If you're interested in attending an upcoming seminar, click below to request additional information.
