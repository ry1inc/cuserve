We are pleased to offer an outstanding opportunity for credit union members to learn about the issues surrounding long term care by working with a seasoned professional. Due to advances in medical technology, our society has been blessed with an increasing average life span. Today, we are living almost twice as long as our ancestors did only 100 years ago. Unfortunately, along with this comes the drawback that the longer we live, the more likely we will need long-term care before we die. For this reason, your credit union wants to make long term care insurance accessible to its members and their family…to preserve your assets and your independence if you are the one of two people who need this type of care.
